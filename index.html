My mission

I started XERA Beauty to create bold, diverse makeup that does more than look good. It heals, nourishes, and empowers. For me, beauty is about expression, authenticity, and accountability.

My story

I have been a performer and makeup artist for over twenty years, from drag to burlesque to theater. I know firsthand how frustrating it is to choose between skincare that feels good and makeup that lasts under lights, through sweat, and during long nights. That is why I decided to build XERA — products that deliver both.

What makes XERA different

  • Performance plus Care: I formulate with the same standards I need on stage. Theater grade longevity that still cares for your skin.
  • Inclusive by Design: I am building this brand for people like me — drag queens, performers, makeup enthusiasts, and everyday wearers who often do not see themselves represented in mainstream beauty.
  • Accountability Over Greenwashing: I do not use the word “sustainable” lightly. My goal is to create refillable systems and packaging that truly work, with honesty and transparency.
  • Fantasy Meets Reality: My first collection, the Lore Line, is inspired by fantasy creatures. It brings storytelling, artistry, and play into everyday makeup.

Coming soon

The first launch will be LipLore, a crystal clear, high shine lip lacquer that is transfer proof, waterproof, and never sticky. I am starting with four shades inspired by fantasy creatures — Dragon’s Blood, Mermaid Tears, Nymph Blessing, and Faerie Dreams.
